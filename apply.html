What

is tech for policy?

Tech4Policy empowers young women to design digital solutions to governance and democratisation challenges in Kosovo’s municipalities.

Over the course of July and August, we will work with young women on identifying, designing, planning, and building digital tools that address everyday governance problems faced by municipalities across Kosovo.

They will discover open source tools employed in design of digital platforms and build prototypes targeting one of the governance problems.

■

Who

can apply?

Young women and girls aged between 18 - 25 with keen interest in using technology for social good.

Selected participants are expected to be available for 3 consecutive days during the month of July or August 2017.

Previous experience or training/education in basic web development will be taken into account, but is not required.

■

Why

should I apply?

At the end of the summer, 2 solutions with the greatest potential for improving local governance will be selected for incubation over the course of 3 months (September - December 2017).

The selected teams will receive financial and technical support to fully develop their digital solutions and will be supervised by ODK and GCK experts until the delivery of the solutions to the recipient municipalities.
